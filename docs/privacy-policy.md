# Super Goode Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

Super Goode is a restaurant discovery app. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information when you use the app.

## Summary

- Super Goode does not create user accounts.
- Super Goode does not collect, sell, rent, or store personal data on a backend controlled by the developer.
- Super Goode does not use analytics SDKs, ad networks, or tracking SDKs.
- Favorites are stored locally on your device.
- Location access is optional and used only to center the map around your current position.

## Information Super Goode Does Not Collect

Super Goode does not ask you to create an account or sign in.

Super Goode does not collect or store personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, payment details, or contacts.

Super Goode does not use advertising identifiers, analytics tracking, or cross-app tracking.

## Location Permission

Super Goode can request foreground location permission so the Map tab can center on your current position and help you browse nearby restaurants.

Location data is used on device for that feature and is not stored by the developer or sent to a developer-controlled server for profiling, analytics, or tracking.

If you do not grant location permission, you can still use the app.

## Favorites

If you save a restaurant as a favorite, the favorite is stored locally on your device using on-device storage.

Favorites are not uploaded to a developer account or synced to a backend service.

## Restaurant Data

Super Goode reads a restaurant dataset packaged with the app or delivered from the app's configured restaurant feed. That dataset describes restaurants and reviews. It is not a user account system and is not used to collect personal information about you.

## External Links and Third-Party Services

Super Goode may open third-party links so you can:

- watch a restaurant review on services such as Instagram or TikTok
- open directions in Google Maps or another maps destination URL

When you open or view third-party content, those third-party services may collect data under their own privacy policies and terms. Super Goode does not control those third-party practices.

## Data Retention

Because Super Goode does not maintain user accounts or a developer-controlled personal-data backend, there is no developer-side user profile to retain or delete.

On-device favorites remain on your device until you remove them or delete the app.

## Children's Privacy

Super Goode is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

If Super Goode's privacy practices change, this Privacy Policy will be updated and the updated version will be posted at the public privacy-policy URL for the app.
